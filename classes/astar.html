A* search

A* extends Dijkstra by adding a heuristic — an estimate of remaining distance to the goal. It prioritises cells with low f = g + h, where g is cost so far and h is the Manhattan distance to the goal. This focuses the search toward the goal, visiting far fewer cells than Dijkstra.
